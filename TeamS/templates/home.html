Welcome to SecPage

A secure social platform for connecting with people

This is an experiment

The aim of this experiment is to study how user password strength can be improved during account registration.

Assume this a social media website where you connect with your family, friends, and colleagues, and follow the instructions.
Please read the instructions carefully before you start.

  1. To start the experiment, click Register to register an account. Enter username and password of your choice.
    For the safety of your information, DO NOT use your personal information as the username (e.g. name, email, phone number, etc.)
    A message indicating password strength will appear as you enter the password.

  2. After successfully registering an account, log in to your account.

  3. After successful login, a link to a survey will appear. Complete the survey to finish the experiment.
    Note: the experiment is not completed until you submit the survey.

You can withdraw from the experiment at any time. However, if you do so, you will automatically waive the right to be debriefed about the purpose of the experiment.
